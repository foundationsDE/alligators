Alligators Are Awesome 🐊

ancient, powerful, and surprisingly chill

Alligators have been around for about 37 million years — they’re literal living dinosaurs. With their armored bodies, stealthy movements, and epic bite, they rule the swamps like bosses.

Cool Gator Facts

  • They can live up to 50 years in the wild.
  • Alligators are mostly found in the southeastern U.S., especially Florida and Louisiana.
  • They can run up to 11 mph on land — but only in short bursts.
  • Alligators don't chew — they swallow chunks whole.
  • Baby alligators make little chirping sounds to call their moms.
